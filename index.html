About Nivaran

Nivaran is a citizen feedback and grievance platform aimed at empowering individuals to report issues and track their resolutions. Our mission is to enhance civic engagement and improve community services.

We believe that every voice matters, and through Nivaran, we aim to bridge the gap between citizens and local authorities.
